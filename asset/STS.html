Our Dream

Our big vision started in a small food stall in Batam. The founders are a couple that came from Bandung , West Java, a city where you can find creativity combined with good food to create an unforgettable dining experience. They couldn’t find the same atmosphere in Batam back then, and really wished to create one someday.
